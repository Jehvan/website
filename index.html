About us

At Zion Coffee & Bar, we believe in creating an experience that blends the best of both worlds—craft coffee by day, and expertly mixed cocktails by night. Located in Aerodrom, our cozy café transforms as the day turns into evening, offering a vibrant space where coffee enthusiasts and cocktail lovers alike can unwind. From the rich aroma of freshly brewed espresso to the sparkle of a well-shaken cocktail, every drink is made with passion and precision. Whether you're here to kickstart your morning or savor a handcrafted cocktail with friends, Zion Coffee & bar is your go-to destination for good times and great drinks. Sip, relax, and enjoy the best of both worlds with us!

CanZ

At Zion Coffee & Bar, you can now take your favorite cocktails and coffee to go with our convenient to-go cans! Say goodbye to boring takeout coffee cups and hello to Canz! Our specialty cold brew and iced coffee blends are brewed to perfection and canned for maximum flavor. Whether it’s a freddo espresso or a decadent Caramel Iced Latte, you’ll get café-quality taste in a sleek, portable can that’s perfect for the road, the office, or anywhere your day takes you.

Enjoy bar-worthy beverages wherever you are, whether it's at a picnic, on a beach day, or simply relaxing at home. Choose from our refreshing cocktails, featuring both signature creations from our exclusive Summer Menu and timeless classics like the Mojito, Negroni, or Margarita. Each drink is freshly mixed with premium ingredients and expertly sealed in our sleek Canz, ensuring you get the perfect cocktail experience anytime, anywhere.
